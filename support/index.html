Support

PATINA - Last updated: May 8, 2026

Need help with PATINA, purchases, ads, restore, or privacy choices? Email the support address below.

a.alameldien@gmail.com

What to include

Please include your device model, OS version, app version if visible, store country or region, and a short description of the issue. Do not send payment card details.

Purchases and restore

Use Restore Purchases inside PATINA first. If restore does not work, include the product you purchased, the approximate purchase date, and the platform store region used for the purchase.

Ads and privacy choices

If your question is about ads or consent, mention your country or region and whether you are trying to reopen Ad Privacy Choices from the Settings screen.

Data deletion

Most PATINA progress is stored locally. Reset progress in Settings if available, or uninstall the app to remove local save data. Contact support for privacy questions or provider-specific guidance.

PATINA is an independent mobile puzzle game by Gaborandi.
