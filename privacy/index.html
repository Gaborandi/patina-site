Privacy Policy

PATINA - Last updated: May 5, 2026

PATINA ("we", "our", or "the app") is a single-player mobile puzzle game. This policy explains what data is stored, what third-party services may collect data, and what choices you have.

1. Data Stored Locally

PATINA stores game progress and preferences locally on your device. This includes completed levels, stars earned, discoveries, settings, daily challenge state, restoration progress, and local entitlement flags. This local save data is not sent to our servers.

2. Third-Party Services

Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to show advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers, ad interaction data, approximate location, device information, and usage data to provide, limit, measure, and improve ads. Personalized advertising is subject to your consent where required. See Google's Privacy Policy.

Apple StoreKit

In-app purchases are processed by Apple through StoreKit. We do not receive or store your payment card details or Apple ID password. Apple handles payment processing, receipts, refunds, and purchase history according to Apple's Privacy Policy.

3. Advertising and Consent

Rewarded ads are optional and only shown when you choose to watch one for an in-game benefit. Interstitial ads, if enabled, appear only between gameplay moments and never during active puzzle play.

On iOS 14.5 and later, Apple's App Tracking Transparency prompt controls whether the app may track activity across other companies' apps and websites for personalized advertising. You can change this in iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking.

In regions where GDPR-style consent applies, the app may show a Google User Messaging Platform consent prompt before requesting ads.

4. Data We Do Not Collect

  • No account is required.
  • We do not collect your name, email address, contacts, photos, or precise location.
  • We do not operate chat, public profiles, or user-generated public posts.
  • We do not sell personal data.

5. Children

PATINA is designed as a general-audience puzzle game. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us and we will take appropriate action.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

Local game data can be deleted by using Reset All Progress in Settings or by uninstalling the app. AdMob and Apple retain data according to their own policies.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, delete, correct, object to, or restrict processing of personal data. Because PATINA stores gameplay progress locally, deleting the app removes local save data. For data handled by Google or Apple, contact those providers directly or use their privacy controls.

8. Changes

We may update this policy. The date at the top of this page reflects the latest version.

9. Contact

For privacy questions, contact a.alameldien@gmail.com.
