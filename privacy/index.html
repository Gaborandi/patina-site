Privacy Policy

PATINA - Last updated: May 8, 2026

This website can translate navigation and summaries for convenience. The full legal policy is provided in English, and the English version controls.

PATINA ("we", "our", or "the app") is a single-player mobile puzzle game. This policy explains what data is stored locally, what third-party services may process data, and what choices you have.

Current launch profile:
  • No account is required to play.
  • Game progress and settings are stored locally on your device.
  • Google AdMob may be used for optional rewarded ads and limited interstitial ads.
  • Google User Messaging Platform manages ad consent where required.
  • Apple App Tracking Transparency controls iOS tracking permission where applicable.
  • Purchases are handled through the platform store. PATINA does not receive payment card details.

1. Data Stored Locally

PATINA stores game progress and preferences locally on your device. This may include completed levels, stars earned, discoveries, cosmetic choices, settings, daily challenge state, restoration progress, power-up stock, and local entitlement flags. Local save data is not sent to our own server.

2. Advertising and Consent

We may use Google AdMob to show advertisements. Rewarded ads are optional and are shown only when you choose to watch one for an in-game benefit. Interstitial ads, if enabled, appear only between gameplay moments and never during active puzzle play.

AdMob may process device identifiers, ad interaction data, approximate location, device information, and usage data to provide, limit, measure, and improve ads. Personalized advertising is subject to your consent where required. See Google's Privacy Policy.

In regions where GDPR-style consent applies, PATINA uses Google's User Messaging Platform to request ad consent before requesting ads. If you decline or limit consent and Google still permits ad requests, PATINA requests non-personalized ads. You can reopen available ad privacy choices from the app's Settings screen.

On iOS 14.5 and later, Apple's App Tracking Transparency prompt controls whether the app may track activity across other companies' apps and websites for personalized advertising. You can change this in iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking.

3. Purchases

In-app purchases are processed by the platform store, such as Apple StoreKit on iOS. The store handles payment processing, receipts, refunds, account-level purchase history, and payment credentials. PATINA does not receive your payment card details or store account password.

PATINA stores local entitlement state needed to unlock purchases such as Remove Ads, Founder Pack, premium themes, cosmetic effects, garden decorations, or power-up packs. Restoring purchases asks the platform store to re-check your owned products.

4. Analytics and Diagnostics

PATINA currently has internal gameplay event names for development and balancing. In development builds, these may be logged locally to the console. No third-party analytics SDK is currently wired into the production app. If a third-party analytics provider is added later, this policy and the app store privacy disclosures should be updated before release.

5. Data We Do Not Collect

  • No account is required.
  • We do not collect your name, email address, contacts, photos, or precise location through PATINA.
  • We do not operate chat, public profiles, or user-generated public posts.
  • We do not sell personal data.

6. Children

PATINA is designed as a general-audience puzzle game. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us and we will take appropriate action.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

Local game data remains on your device until you reset progress, delete app data, or uninstall the app. AdMob, Apple, Google Play, and other platform services retain data according to their own policies.

You can delete local PATINA progress by using Reset All Progress in Settings, if available, or by uninstalling the app. For data handled by Google, Apple, or Google Play, use those providers' privacy tools or contact those providers directly.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, delete, correct, object to, or restrict processing of personal data. Because PATINA's gameplay progress is stored locally, deleting the app or resetting progress removes local save data. For privacy questions, contact us using the address below.

9. Changes

We may update this policy. The date at the top of this page reflects the latest version.

10. Contact

For privacy, support, correction, deletion, or purchase-support requests, contact a.alameldien@gmail.com.

PATINA is an independent mobile puzzle game by Gaborandi.
